FAQ

How do I suggest a new transmitter?

  • First you need to identify the satellite which carries the transmitter. Do that by searching on the main search field and selecting the satellite you would like to add a new transmitter in.
  • Make sure that the transmitter is not already listed under the satellite. Double check for other Modes that might include the information you would like to add.
  • Click on the small plus icon on the upper right corner of the satellite.
  • Complete the fields to the best of your knowledge/research.
  • Make sure to include the citation URL, pointing to the web page you retrieved this information, so that the reviewers can confirm the submission.
  • Click submit and you are done!
  • SatNOGS DB reviewers will be checking your submission as soon as possible to approve it!

How do I suggest an edit to a transmitter?

  • First you need to identify the satellite which carries the transmitter.
  • Do that by searching on the main search field and selecting the satellite that carries the transmitter you would like to edit.
  • Double check for other Modes that might include the information you would like to add.
  • Click on the small plus icon on the upper right corner of the transmitter.
  • Complete/Edit the fields to the best of your knowledge/research.
  • Make sure to include the citation URL, pointing to the web page you retrieved this information, so that the reviewers can confirm the submission.
  • Click submit and you are done!
  • SatNOGS DB reviewers will be checking your submission as soon as possible to approve it!

The satellite I am looking is not there.

  • Not all satellites are in SatNOGS DB yet. We prioritized the most common ones that we have transmitter information for. We need your help to expand our database!
  • Go to our Community Forum and open a new thread requesting a satellite addition, with additional info (links and citations) about it.
  • A DB reviewer will answer promptly and add the satellite to the DB so you can suggest transmitter information.
